ECONOS 2020

Welcome to the 19th European Conference On Non-Linear Optical Spectroscopy (ECONOS 2020), the annual meeting devoted to diverse topics in the field of nonlinear optical spectroscopy, including coherent Raman scattering, novel laser sources, microscopy, imaging, combustion, ultra-fast and vibrational spectroscopy.
